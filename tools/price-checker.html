NZ Price Tracker

Track product prices over time from NZ retail sites. History stored in your browser.

Supported sites & how to add items

🛒 The Warehouse — thewarehouse.co.nz

Paste any product URL. Price is read from the page's Schema.org product data (JSON-LD).

Example:
https://www.thewarehouse.co.nz/p/gamenote-wired-gaming-mouse-black/R3016083.html

Current price: $24.00 (on special at time of writing)

🥦 Pak'nSave — paknsave.co.nz

Uses their live decorateProducts API. You need to supply your store's UUID once — it is saved per item.

How to find your Store ID:

  1. Open any Pak'nSave product page in Chrome / Edge
  2. Press F12 → Network tab → reload the page
  3. Filter by decorateProducts
  4. Copy the UUID from the request URL:
    …/store/xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx/decorateProducts

Example URL:
https://www.paknsave.co.nz/shop/product/5283751_ea_000pns?name=sunsilk-detox-for-men-shampoo

Example Store ID (Napier Pak'nSave):
49037947-e450-49bf-ba1f-a94551c79a72

Price is returned as integer cents — 799 = $7.99

ℹ️ Storage note

Price history is stored in your browser's localStorage (not cookies — cookies are limited to 4 KB; localStorage supports several MB). Data stays on your device and is never sent anywhere.

One price entry is recorded per item per day. Prices are only updated when you load this page.

⚠️ Legality / fair use

This is a personal price-tracking tool for your own shopping. Fetching uses the public CORS proxy corsproxy.io. Retail sites' Terms of Service may restrict automated access — use reasonably and for personal use only.

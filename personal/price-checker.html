How it works & known limitations

❌ Known not to work

  • Mitre 10 — Cloudflare blocks all CORS proxy IPs
  • PB Tech — WAF blocks all CORS proxies
  • Noel Leeming — WAF blocks all CORS proxies
  • Kmart — Akamai CDN blocks all CORS proxies
  • Harvey Norman — Imperva/Incapsula bot challenge
  • Bunnings — Cloudflare bot protection
  • Mighty Ape — Cloudflare (HTTP 403 via proxy)
  • Pak'nSave — SPA; price not in static HTML

ℹ️ Storage

History is stored in localStorage — it stays on your device and is never sent anywhere. One entry is recorded per item per day, updated on page load.

⚠️ Fair use

Personal use only. Fetching is done via the public CORS proxy corsproxy.io. Retail sites' Terms of Service may restrict automated access — use responsibly.

🗑️ Deleting data

Use the ✕ button on a card to remove that item. To wipe everything at once:

  1. Press F12 → Application tab (Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  2. Expand Local Storage → click https://david-fear.github.io
  3. Find the key pricetracker_v1 and press Delete
